# Figure 1

[Figure 1](../Summary/figure_1.png) is comprised of several components.

Figure 1A depicts the simulated ground truth of our three-epoch demographic scenario, consisting of a forward-in-time ancestral epoch contracting to a bottleneck epoch and then expanding to the current epoch.

Figure 1B depicts our downsampling strategy, varying sample size by increments of 10 diploid individuals starting at 10 and ending at a maximum sample size of 800.

Figure 1C depits how simple two-epoch inference can only capture one evolutionary signal, either the ancient contraction or the recent expansion. Whichever sample-size dependent evolutionary signal we infer is considered the "dominant evolutionary signal" for that sample size.

This figure was made in Microsoft Powerpoint. Figure components can be found in [`../Summary/figures.pptx`](../Summary/figures.pptx).
